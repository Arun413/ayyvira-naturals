Our Story

Born from a Love of Nature & Skin

Ayvira Naturals was founded by Priya Venkataraman in the heart of Tamil Nadu, inspired by her grandmother's age-old skincare rituals using neem, turmeric, and rose water. What started as a kitchen project became a movement.

Every product tells the story of a plant, a tradition, and a promise — to care for your skin with the same reverence we have for the earth.

  • Sourced directly from organic farms
  • Dermatologist tested formulations
  • Free from harmful chemicals & microplastics
