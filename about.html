How It Started

A Kitchen, A Garden, and a Dream

Ayvira Naturals was born in the summer of 2018 in a small kitchen in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu. Founder Krishnaveni Krishnan, a botanist by training, watched her grandmother blend fresh turmeric, neem leaves, and rose petals every morning as part of her skincare ritual — a ritual that had been passed down for generations.

"My paati's skin was luminous at 80. Her secret wasn't expensive creams — it was the earth itself." — Krishnaveni Krishnan, Founder

Frustrated by a beauty industry flooded with chemicals and empty promises, Priya began recreating those traditional formulations, combining them with modern dermatological research. After two years of testing and refinement, Ayvira Naturals launched with its first collection of five products.

Today, with over 5,000 happy customers across India, the brand remains committed to the same principles it started with: transparency, purity, and deep respect for the natural world.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Every decision we make — from ingredient sourcing to packaging design — is guided by these core beliefs.

01

🌱 Purity First

We believe your skin deserves only the best nature has to offer. Every ingredient is rigorously vetted — no fillers, no toxins, no compromises. Our formulas are as clean as they come.

02

🌍 Earth Stewardship

We source sustainably, work with fair-trade farms, and use biodegradable or recycled packaging. Our goal is to leave the planet cleaner than we found it — one bottle at a time.

03

🐾 Cruelty-Free Always

No animal testing. Ever. We are certified cruelty-free and proudly vegan. We believe beauty and compassion are inseparable.

04

🤝 Community & Heritage

We partner with local women-led cooperatives in Tamil Nadu, preserving traditional botanical knowledge and ensuring fair livelihoods for the communities that inspire us.

05

🔬 Science + Tradition

We honour Ayurvedic wisdom and validate it through modern dermatological research. Our team of scientists ensures every formulation is both effective and safe for all skin types.

06

💚 Radical Transparency

Full ingredient disclosure on every label. No greenwashing, no hidden chemicals. We tell you exactly what's in every product and why — because you deserve to know.
